Our Story
DK Jhula —
A Home Business
Built on Craft
Owner & Artisan
Daxaben K. Prajapati

DK Jhula is a small home-based business run with passion from Anand, Gujarat. Every jhula and home decor item is handcrafted using premium resham doori — no factory, no machines. Just skilled hands, a loving heart, and 15+ years of experience.

All products are fully washable and come with a rope warranty. We customize colour, design, and size for any order. Our jhulas hold 150–200 kg and are safe for all ages — from babies to grandparents.
